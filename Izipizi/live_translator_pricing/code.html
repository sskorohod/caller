Billing Questions

How does the "Pay Per Minute" actually work?

You purchase credits in advance. When you start a translation session, we track the usage down to the second and deduct from your balance only for the active translation time.

Do my credits ever expire?

No. Whether you use them today or next year, your credits remain in your account until used. We do not have "use it or lose it" policies.

Can I get a refund for unused credits?

Yes, we offer a full refund for any unused credit balance within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked.

Are there any hidden connection fees?

None. You pay exactly $0.15 per minute of translation. We don't charge for opening the app, setting up languages, or storage of your transcripts.
